AKAN NAMES

Akan names are names from Ghan that the parents give to there children according to the day of the week they were born.

As a user you will be required to enter your Birth year,Month you were born,the date of birth and also you gender.So as you enter you birth information you will be able to see you Akan name and you will also see the day you were born.
